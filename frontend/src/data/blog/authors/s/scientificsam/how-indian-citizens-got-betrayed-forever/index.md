# India's toxic loop

<p style="text-align: justify;">
Most of India's parties, politicians, governments, and Private organizations play a mind game continually to break every Indian citizen and keep them occupied, which an Indian citizen can never escape from childhood to adulthood.

<p style="text-align: justify;">
The typical environment a child born into India is not filled with awe and curiosity about the wonders of the universe, Biology, engineering, Mathematical and physical paradoxes, Financial and Relationship knowledge.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
A child is born into ideologies, identity, Religion, caste, foreign forces, and very rigid beliefs about everything you can think of. Most of the Indian assembly, from central to local, is filled with these ideologies instead of curiosity and problem-solving. In fact, most of the people doesn't even know how to identify the problem that they currently have without biases and rigid beliefs. Most of them doesn't know what a win-win game is(A non zero-sum game). They always think in primordial logics, and they fight personally and politically, and people love it. This is how a glorious roman empire fell down.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
From there, the child pickups these ideologies and grows into adulthood and makes them more powerful, and this loop continues, even among scientist and educated people. Even in the so-called educated communities in southern states of India, the vote is based on these ideologies, not based on education and contribution to society. That is why in the Andhra Pradesh state, an IAS officer like Jayaprakash Narayan won't get the votes at all.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
Most of us can't think much. We have to be fixated on some ideology instead of going based on the Data and Context. It is not our fault alone, the environment is very harsh, rude, and aggressive 24/7. We want to fixate on something like, for example, capitalism, western ideology, communism, or some religion, caste, or some kind of business ideology. We can't separate a person from his thoughts, his actions, and his context. We go after a person, a god, or some kind of rigid identity where we can blame and move on with our lives. We can't hold multiple conflicts in the memory and make an analysis based on that. We can only change the environment by first identifying the environment we are in. Then we can try to change the environment by following best practices or at least be smart about what we can control and what we cannot.
</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">
To build a nation, in the Indian assembly, there should be civil engineers, software engineers, nationalists, human philosophers, and all other kinds of engineers and scientists from various engineering fields and subfields, but what we have is movie actors, ideologists, rowdy boys, and girls. Unless the components of the assembly buliding changes, India will deteriorate more and more.
</p>
